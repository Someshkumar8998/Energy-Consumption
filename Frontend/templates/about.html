About The System

Energy Consumption is a state-of-the-art predictive analytics platform designed to optimize energy consumption in residential and commercial sectors. We leverage the power of Artificial Intelligence to provide actionable insights for a sustainable future.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and organizations with accurate, real-time energy forecasting. By understanding consumption patterns, we can reduce waste, lower operational costs, and contribute significantly to global energy conservation efforts.

Our Vision

We envision a world where energy management is autonomous and intelligent. A future where smart grids and IoT devices communicate seamlessly to balance load, integrate renewable sources, and ensure grid stability for everyone.

Technology Stack

Machine Learning

Powered by Random Forest Regression for high-accuracy time-series forecasting. Utilizing Scikit-Learn and Pandas for robust data processing.

Backend Core

Built on Python Flask, ensuring a lightweight yet powerful server environment capable of handling real-time API requests and model serving.

Modern Frontend

A responsive interface crafted with HTML5, Bootstrap 5, and vanilla JavaScript for dynamic interactivity and smooth user experience.

Data Visualization

Interactive charts and real-time dashboards to visualize energy trends, making complex data understandable and actionable.
